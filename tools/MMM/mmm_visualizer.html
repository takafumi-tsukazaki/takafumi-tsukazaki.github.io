Calculation Logic
x_imme[t] = x[t] × w[0]（current-period instant effect, diagonal component）
x_carry[t] = x_decayed[t] − x_imme[t]（accumulated past contributions）
sat_total = S(x_decayed)、sat_carry = S(x_carry | x_decayed)
sat_imme = sat_total − sat_carry
Immediate% = sum(sat_imme) / sum(sat_total)
