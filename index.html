MMM Function Visualizer

A visualization tool for Adstock and Saturation functions used in Robyn, an MMM tool. It enables users to view the functional shapes and verify the combination of functions alongside the resulting Immediate vs. Carryover ratio.

OLS Visualizer

A visualization tool for the geometry and collinearity diagnostics of OLS linear regression. It shows OLS as the orthogonal projection of y onto the column space C(X) — the residual e ⊥ C(X) and how the coefficients β explode under multicollinearity while the fit (ŷ, R²) stays unchanged — together with a micro-anatomy of multicollinearity: the correlation matrix heatmap, the singular values of X (SVD) and the condition number κ, the VIF of each variable, the near-null direction in which variables cancel, and the parameter uncertainty ellipse that stretches as collinearity grows. For simplicity, the symmetry ρ(x₁,x₃) = ρ(x₂,x₃) is assumed.

Subdifferential Visualizer

This tool is an interactive visualizer for building intuition about the subdifferential (∂f) of convex functions. The subdifferential generalizes the derivative to a set-valued object that is defined even at non-differentiable points (kinks), representing the slopes of all supporting lines (hyperplanes) that touch the graph from below. In 1D mode, three linked panels — the fan of supporting lines, the monotone graph of the subdifferential map x ↦ ∂f(x), and the proximal operator with sparsity — let you feel how ∂f is a single point at smooth points but spreads into an interval at kinks, and how the optimality condition 0 ∈ ∂f(x⋆) characterizes a minimizer. In 2D mode, it visualizes the epigraph surface with a supporting hyperplane, the subdifferential set in gradient space (a box for ℓ₁, a disk for ℓ₂), and LASSO-style sparsity regions, showing why the geometry of the subdifferential produces exact zeros (sparse solutions). You can drag the evaluation point and tune the parameters (λ, v) with sliders, turning abstract convex-analysis concepts into something you can explore by hand.

凸関数の劣微分(subdifferential) ∂f を直感的に理解するためのインタラクティブなビジュアライザです。劣微分は、微分不可能な点(kink)でも定義できる「微分の集合値への一般化」です。1次元モードでは、関数と接線・劣微分写像 x↦∂f(x) の単調グラフ・proximal operatorとスパース性の3パネルを連動表示し、滑らかな点では1点・kink では区間に広がる様子や、最適性条件 0∈∂f(x⋆) を確認できます。さらに2次元モードでは、エピグラフ曲面と支持超平面・勾配空間における劣微分集合・LASSO的なスパース性領域を可視化しています。評価点やパラメータ(λ, v)をドラッグ・スライダーで操作することができます。
